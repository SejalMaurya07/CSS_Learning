Data Science - A data science course is a structured educational program designed to teach individuals the foundational concepts, tools, and techniques of data science. These data science courses typically cover a wide range of topics, including statistics, programming, machine learning, data visualization, and data analysis. They are suitable for beginners with little to no prior experience in data science, as well as professionals looking to expand their skills or transition into a data-related role.
